Standard Operating Procedure: Hot Swap

  1. Open Spare Suction: Flood the spare pump with liquid to protect the mechanical seals.
  2. Start Spare Motor: Spin up the spare pump against its closed discharge valve.
  3. Open Spare Discharge: Establish forward flow through the spare pump.
  4. Close Primary Discharge: Pinch off the bad pump to prevent fluid from slamming backward.
  5. Stop Primary Motor: Shut down the vibrating equipment.
  6. Close Primary Suction: Fully isolate the bad pump so maintenance can safely pull the casing.
