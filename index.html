Parent's Corner: Beyond the ABCs

Fueling Curiosity isn't just for toddlers. The unique "Beyond the ABCs" section gives this book exceptional shelf life as your child grows.

While the main pages focus on engaging personification to introduce complex concepts, the back of the book dives deeper into the actual science and engineering principles. This allows parents to learn right alongside their children, fostering a shared environment of STEM discovery.

A Note to Parents

Hi, I'm Taylor. As a Logistics Manager and Refinery Engineer, I wanted to bridge the gap between the industrial world I work in and the curious minds of our children.

I wrote this book to make chemical engineering accessible, friendly, and fun. With over 60,000 refinery employees in the US, I hope this book serves as a perfect way to share what we do with our families!
