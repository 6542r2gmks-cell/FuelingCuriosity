Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fueling Curiosity. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how we handle your data when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

We collect personal information, specifically your email address, only when you voluntarily submit it to us. This occurs when you opt-in to download digital products (like our bonus coloring pages) or when you contact us directly to request a bulk order quote or ask a question.

Automatically Collected Information:

We use standard website analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that automatically collect anonymous data when you visit our site, including:

  • Your device type and browser
  • General location data (such as city or region)
  • How you interact with our website (pages viewed, links clicked)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver requested digital products directly to you.
  • Respond to your direct inquiries, including bulk order and school sponsorship requests.
  • Send you occasional updates about new books, educational resources, or related projects (you may opt out of these at any time).
  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve the experience for families and educators.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services & Data Processors

We use trusted third-party services to help operate our website securely:

  • Mailchimp (Intuit): We use Mailchimp to securely store the email addresses you provide and manage our newsletter communications.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to monitor site traffic. You can opt out of this tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • GitHub Pages: Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs including IP addresses.
  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare to optimize performance and enhance the security of our website. As part of providing these services, Cloudflare may process the following data:
    • IP addresses
    • Geolocation data (based on IP address)
    • Device and browser information
    • Requests made to our website (e.g., pages visited, files accessed)
    Cloudflare collects and processes this data to protect against malicious activity, serve cached content, optimize load times, and improve the overall security of our website. For more details, please review their Privacy Policy.

5. Affiliate Disclosure

Fueling Curiosity participates in affiliate programs, including the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. This means that some links on our website may be affiliate links.

If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. These commissions help support the operation and continued development of this website. We only recommend products we believe are educational, useful, and aligned with our mission.

6. Children's Privacy

While Fueling Curiosity is an educational book for children, this website and its associated data collection forms are intended strictly for use by parents, educators, and adults. We do not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we learn that personal information from a child under 13 has been collected, we will promptly delete that data from our records.

7. Your Privacy Rights & Opting Out

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You may unsubscribe from our email list by clicking the "unsubscribe" link included at the bottom of our marketing emails, or by contacting us directly to request removal.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, how we handle your data, or our affiliate relationships, please contact us at hello@fuelingcuriosity.com.
